What are Macros?

Macros (short for macro-nutrients), are essential substances that are required for: energy, growth, and development. There are three categories of macros: proteins, fats, and carbohydrates.

How much of each macronutrient should you consume everyday?

Depending on your weight and goals, we can tailor these macros to determine how much your body needs. Traditionally, it's a split of 30% protein, 25% fat, and 45% carbs.
